Club Table Tennis at UVA strives to provide an opportunity for members of the University community to practice and play recreational and competitive table tennis throughout the year. The club organizes ping pong-related events to foster an appreciation and awareness of the sport. Anyone with an interest in table tennis is welcome to join. All skill levels welcome!

Check out our constitution for more details!
